I'm Sudeeksha

What's cooking mon cher? I’m Sudeeksha, the world’s most accomplished expert in turning simple tasks like wasin dishes into elaborate misadventures. I have a unique approach to problem-solving (mostly involving chaos, duct tape, and NO sheer luck). Whether it's writing documentary about my life so that my kids can see it and be like "Oh my god mom! You were this cool?!", dancing so much that my calories burn (C'mon! It's still a win-win situation.), proposing the weirdest ideas and still having fun with everyone or actually participating in a rifle shooting competition and winning a prize! (Even tho I've got glasses).

I mean you get it, I get things done quite easily, only after my mom lectures me (Haha). So, I have a very diverse kinds of hobbies. I like to paint, read, dance, I also like fashion designing, swimming (That's kind of random I know), playing badminton and doing experiments with my brother (Which I don't want to talk about...) Oh did I meantion fashion designing?

Now what do I talk about? Oh yeah, I got it, an inspirational speech. To sum it all up, I'm way too clumsy to be alive. I make the dumbest decisions and later on I'm like "Why did I do that?". So I am very glad to have my parents (and my brother sometimes) around and help me in decision making. At first their oipinions seem irrational, but they make a lot more sense than our own decisions. And as the poem "The Road Not Taken" from my english textbook says, "Decisions making is a crucial stepping stone of our life." it's like building a bridge without a structure or layout. I don't want to yap the entire day but let's end it the way my dad does, "I don't want to lecture you on such a good day so let's wrap it up!". Thank you dad (and mom).
